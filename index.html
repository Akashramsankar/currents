DAILY WORD CIRCUIT

How to Play

🎯 Goal: Today's clue has a hidden answer. Slide the grid to line up the answer's letters, then trace them in one unbroken line — in as few moves as possible.
📖
Read the clue. The blanks below it show how many letters each word has, e.g. (4, 5).
↔️
Shift the grid. Swipe any row or column — or tap the edge arrows — to slide letters. They wrap around the edges. Each shift counts as one move.
⚡
Follow the sparks. A glowing line appears between two neighbouring tiles when they're next to each other in the answer. Its arrow points from the earlier letter to the next, showing which way to trace. A violet spark means that link jumps from the end of one word to the start of the next. Use them to line up the whole phrase into one connected chain. Repeated letters? Sparks mark only the correct copy — if a letter sits next to the right neighbour but stays dark, it's the wrong one for that spot.
✏️
Trace the answer. Tap "⚡ Trace Phrase", then drag from the first letter to the last — up, down, left or right only (no diagonals, no reusing a tile). Spell the answer to win.
💡
Stuck? The Hint button reveals one answer letter at a time.
⛳
Beat Par. Par is the target number of moves, set from the puzzle's fastest possible solve. Solve it in fewer for a Birdie or Eagle! The ★ stars by the clue rate how tough today's puzzle is.
